Where Your Money Goes

We believe in radical transparency. Here's exactly how we use every dollar:

  • 72% Adaptive equipment & bikes
  • 12% Professional fitting & follow-up
  • 8% Clinical assessments & matching
  • 5% Outreach & awareness
  • 3% Administration

92 cents of every dollar goes directly to getting bikes to children. We keep overhead minimal because every dollar matters.
