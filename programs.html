Our Programs

How we get adaptive bikes into the hands of the children who need them

How to Apply

Our process is simple, personal, and designed to move quickly

1

Submit an Application

Fill out our online form with basic information about your child, their diagnosis, and their mobility needs. It takes about 15 minutes. A parent, guardian, or therapist can apply.

2

Clinical Assessment

We'll request a brief letter from your child's physical therapist, occupational therapist, or physician confirming the need for adaptive equipment and recommending a type of bike.

3

Equipment Matching

Our team works with certified adaptive technology specialists to identify the best equipment for your child. We consider their diagnosis, size, strength, goals, and growth potential.

4

Funding & Fitting

Once matched, we fund the equipment, fully or with significant assistance based on family need. The bike is professionally fitted to your child, with adjustments as they grow.

5

Ride Day!

Your child receives their bike. We provide a brief orientation and riding tips. Then it's time to ride! We follow up at 30, 90, and 180 days to make sure everything is working perfectly.

Eligibility

We serve children and teens ages 2–18 who have a physical, neurological, or developmental disability that prevents them from safely using a standard bicycle.

Common qualifying conditions include:

  • Cerebral palsy
  • Spina bifida
  • Muscular dystrophy
  • Down syndrome
  • Autism spectrum disorder (with motor challenges)
  • Spinal cord injuries
  • Amputation or limb differences
  • Traumatic brain injury
  • Visual impairment (tandem bikes)
  • Other conditions affecting mobility or balance

Financial need is a factor but not a barrier. We use a sliding scale and work with every family. No child is turned away solely because of income.

What We Cover

Equipment and services included in our grants

Adaptive Bikes & Trikes

Full funding for adaptive tricycles, hand cycles, tandem bikes, and specialty two-wheelers from leading manufacturers.

Seating & Support

Custom trunk supports, lateral supports, harnesses, headrests, and positioning accessories for safe, comfortable riding.

Modifications

Pedal adaptations, hand grips, steering modifications, brake extensions, and other custom adjustments.

Professional Fitting

Initial fitting by a certified specialist plus follow-up adjustments as your child grows. Included at no extra cost.

Helmets & Safety Gear

Adaptive helmets and safety accessories included with every bike to ensure safe riding from day one.

Growth Program

Children grow fast. Our Growth Program provides size upgrades and exchanges as your child outgrows their equipment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the process take?

Most families receive their child's bike within 6–10 weeks of submitting a complete application. Complex cases or custom builds may take longer.

Does insurance cover adaptive bikes?

Unfortunately, most insurance plans do not cover adaptive recreational equipment. That's exactly why we exist. We encourage families to check with their provider, but our program is designed to fill this gap.

Is there a cost to families?

We use a sliding scale based on financial need. Many families receive full funding. Others contribute a portion based on their ability. No family is turned away for inability to pay.

Can a therapist apply on behalf of a family?

Yes! Therapists, teachers, case workers, and other professionals can submit applications with the family's consent. We encourage professional referrals.

What if my child outgrows the bike?

Our Growth Program allows families to exchange or upgrade equipment as their child grows. Contact us when it's time and we'll work with you to find the right next step.

Do you serve adults?

Currently our programs focus on children and teens ages 2–18. We hope to expand to adult services in the future. In the meantime, we're happy to connect adults with other resources.

What areas do you serve?

We serve families nationwide across the United States. Equipment is shipped directly or coordinated with a local fitting specialist in your area.

Ready to Get Started?

Applications are accepted year-round. There's no wrong time to apply.
