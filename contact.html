Partner With Us

We work with businesses, foundations, therapy centers, schools, and community organizations. Partnership opportunities include:

  • Corporate sponsorship & employee giving programs
  • Equipment manufacturer partnerships
  • Therapy center referral networks
  • Community event co-hosting
  • School & district partnerships
  • Foundation grants & funding

Contact us at partnerships@pedalsofhope.org to explore how we can work together.

Pedals of Hope
