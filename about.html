About Pedals of Hope

Our mission, our team, and the people behind the wheels

Our Mission

Pedals of Hope exists to ensure that every child, regardless of physical ability or financial circumstance, has access to the adaptive cycling equipment they need to ride a bike.

We believe bike riding is more than recreation. It builds confidence, strengthens muscles, improves coordination, and most importantly, lets kids be kids alongside their friends and siblings.

What We Believe

The values that guide everything we do

Inclusion First

Every child deserves to participate. Disability should never mean exclusion from the simple joys of childhood.

Dignity Always

We treat every family with respect. Our process is simple, private, and judgment-free. No family should feel embarrassed asking for help.

Right Fit, Every Time

We don't do one-size-fits-all. Every child is matched with equipment specifically suited to their abilities, goals, and body.
